Admissions Information

Begin your academic journey at Garsen Technical and Vocational College by following these steps to apply.

Application Deadlines

  • ALL Semester: ONGOING

Admission Criteria

We assess applications based on the following criteria:

  • Academic achievements
  • Personal essays
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Interview performance (if applicable)

For detailed requirements, please visit our Admissions Portal.

How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to apply:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit all required documents.
  3. Pay the application fee (if required).
  4. Attend an interview (if required).
  5. Receive your admission decision.
Apply Now

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • High school transcripts
  • Personal statement
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Proof of English proficiency (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photographs

Fees & Scholarships

Tuition fees vary by program. We offer a range of scholarships to support our students:

  • Merit-Based Scholarships: Awarded to students with outstanding academic achievements.
  • Need-Based Scholarships: Designed to assist students who demonstrate financial need.
  • Specialty Scholarships: For students pursuing specific fields of study.

For more information on fees and scholarships, please visit our Fees & Scholarships page.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • What are the admission requirements? Admission requirements vary by program. Please refer to the Admissions Portal for detailed information.
  • How do I apply for a scholarship? Scholarships are automatically considered with your application. You can also apply for specific scholarships through the Scholarships page.
  • When will I receive my admission decision? Admission decisions are typically made within 4-6 weeks after the application deadline.
  • Can I defer my admission? Yes, deferrals may be granted under certain circumstances. Please contact the admissions office for more information.
