Our History

GARSEN TECHNICAL AND VOCATIONAL COLLEGE is a governmental college originally located in Abaganda, Garsen Constituency in Tana River County, approximately 40 km from Garsen town. In 2021, GTVC admitted its first 14 students, and today, the enrolment has grown to over 130 students. Currently, the institution is located near Minjila Trading Centre, adjacent to the Tana Delta Deputy County Commissioner's office.
